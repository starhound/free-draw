Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Free Draw mobile application (the "App") developed by Wesley Reid ("we", "us", or "our").

1. Data Collection

Free Draw is designed with children's privacy as the absolute top priority. We do not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal information, telemetry, usage data, or analytics.

2. Internet Connectivity

The App functions entirely offline. It does not connect to the internet, does not use external APIs, and does not serve advertisements.

3. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Our App complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). Because we do not collect any personal data from our users, we do not require parental consent to use the application.

4. Local Storage

Any artwork or drawings created within the App are stored entirely locally on the user's device and are never uploaded to any server.

5. Changes to this Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the Amazon Appstore developer contact page.
