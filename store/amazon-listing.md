# Amazon Appstore Listing Draft

## App name

Free Draw

## Short description

A simple drawing and coloring studio for kids.

## Full description

Free Draw gives kids a clean tablet-sized canvas for drawing, coloring, and playful mark making. It includes blank paper, 280+ original coloring pages, a curated Animal Friends set, a Classic Sheets pack from public-domain sources, bright colors, a rainbow brush, an eraser, a fill tool, mirror drawing, and simple stamps.

The app is designed for families who want a calm creative space without accounts, ads, social features, or in-app purchases. Drawings stay on the device, and kids can save a picture when they are done.

## Feature bullets

- Touch-first drawing canvas
- 280+ original non-IP coloring book pages
- Curated Animal Friends sheets
- Classic Sheets pack with local source metadata
- Brush, rainbow brush, eraser, and fill tools
- Stamps and mirror drawing
- Undo, redo, clear, and save
- Offline after installation
- No ads, accounts, analytics, or in-app purchases
