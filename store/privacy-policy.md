# Free Draw Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 6, 2026

Free Draw is a drawing and coloring app for kids and families. Free Draw does not collect, sell, rent, or share personal information.

Free Draw does not collect personal information, account information, location data, contacts, photos, audio, advertising identifiers, analytics data, crash analytics, or device identifiers.

The app stores the current drawing, selected page, and drawing settings locally on the device so the picture can be restored. This data stays on the device unless a user chooses to export or share a saved picture through device features outside Free Draw.

Free Draw is designed to avoid collecting children's personal information. The app does not include accounts, social features, ads, in-app purchases, push notifications, unrestricted web content, analytics SDKs, or third-party tracking SDKs.

Free Draw does not send drawing data or personal information to the developer or to third parties. If Free Draw is installed from an app store, that store may process installation, purchase, device, or storefront data under its own privacy policy.

For support or privacy questions, use the support link listed on the app store page or visit https://github.com/starhound/free-draw/issues.
