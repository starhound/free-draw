Free Draw Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 6, 2026

Free Draw is designed for kids and families. The app does not collect, sell, or share personal information.

Drawings, selected colors, and the current coloring page are stored locally on the device so the picture can be restored. Saved pictures are exported only when the user chooses to save them.

The app does not include accounts, advertising, analytics SDKs, location tracking, push notifications, social features, or in-app purchases.

When Free Draw is installed from an app store, that store may process installation, purchase, device, or storefront data under its own policies. Free Draw does not send drawing data or personal information to the developer.

Contact: use the support contact listed on the app store page.
